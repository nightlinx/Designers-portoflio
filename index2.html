Who am I?

I am a Computer Science student interested in design. I have always had fun in drawing, sketching and hand-making. Aside from this I really enjoy reading and learning about psychology.

UX & UI design joins all the things that I love – that's why I am here!

How I work?

Most of my prototypes and designs I am making in my favourite interface design tool – Figma. Now I am learning Axure. When I am creating design for the whole system I am going through UX process.

In most of projects I work in team – not only as an UI designer. I have also experience in software design: creating data base schemas, gathering user requirements, designing UML diagrams, writing use case scenarios and testing usability.

Can I code?

Yes, I can. I have experience with developing web applications. For the short period of time I was working as a front-end developer. I've learnt HTML, CSS, JS and now I am learning SASS and Vue.js – all essential stuff that can make my sites real!
